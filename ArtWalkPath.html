Art Walk Path

About the Path

Wander through a curated stretch of Forest Park where creativity extends beyond gallery walls. The Art Walk Path invites you to explore the connection between nature, sculpture, and architecture as you travel from the Saint Louis Art Museum to the Jewel Box. Along the way, you will encounter moments of artistic discovery, some hidden in plain sight, others revealed through playful clues.

Follow the trail, solve light riddles, and let curiosity guide your route. This path takes approximately 20 to 30 minutes to complete and is designed for all ages and experience levels. Complete the trail to earn a limited edition trinket, created in collaboration with the Saint Louis Art Museum and the Jewel Box, your reward for seeing the park through an artistic lens.
