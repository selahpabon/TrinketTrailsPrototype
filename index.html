About Trinket Trails

Explore Forest Park like never before with Trinket Trails, an interactive trail experience that guides visitors through landmarks, stories, and hidden moments using maps, riddles, and collectible rewards.

Complete trails, collect digital stamps, and earn real-world trinkets that tie each journey back to the art, ecology, and history of the park.

Win Prizes!

Every trail leaves you with something to remember it by. Trinkets are uniquely tied to each experience and celebrate the landmarks, ecosystems, and stories you encounter on the way.

With support from local partners and Forest Park Forever, each collectible becomes a small reminder that exploration can leave a lasting imprint.

How to Win?

1

Pick the Trinket Trail you’d like to complete.

2

Go to the starting point and launch the trail guide on your phone.

3

Scan the QR code to open the Trail Tracker in the Forest Park app.

4

Follow clues, solve prompts, and have fun as you move through the hunt.

5

Scan the final QR code to confirm you’ve completed the trail.

6

Receive a stamp on your Trinket Trail passport and unlock your keepsake.

7

Head to the visitor center to claim your trinket and start the next trail.

Once you complete all of the trails and fill your Trinket Trail passport, you’ll unlock the Ultimate Trinket.
